## Best Practices

### ‚úÖ **DO**
- Use high contrast colors (dark on light, or light on dark)
- Test QR codes before mass printing
- Leave margins for better scanning
- Use SVG for physical materials
- Include venue branding nearby

### ‚ùå **DON'T**
-  Use similar foreground and background colors
- Make QR codes too small (minimum 2cm √ó 2cm for print)
- Remove margins entirely
- Use low contrast color combinations
- Forget to test scan before distribution

## Color Contrast Guidelines

### Good Combinations:
- Black on White (Classic)
- Cyan on Dark (#00f0ff on #0a0a0f)
- Gold on Dark (#ffd700 on #1a1a1f)
- Dark on Light (#1a1a1a on #f5f5f5)

### Avoid:
- Light on Light
- Dark on Dark
- Low contrast combinations
- Neon on Neon

## Support

### Troubleshooting

**QR Code won't scan:**
- Check color contrast
- Increase size
- Add margins
- Test with multiple scanner apps

**Export not working:**
- Check browser permissions
- Try different format
- Refresh page and try again

**Colors not matching:**
- Verify hex codes
- Use color picker
- Check preset themes
